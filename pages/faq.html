Yes! All our frames can be fitted with prescription lenses. During checkout, you can:

  • Enter your prescription manually
  • Upload a photo of your prescription
  • Have us contact your eye doctor

We work with licensed opticians to ensure accurate lens crafting.

Your prescription should include:

  • OD/OS: Right eye/Left eye values
  • Sphere (SPH): Lens power needed
  • Cylinder (CYL): Astigmatism correction (if needed)
  • Axis: Angle of astigmatism correction
  • PD: Pupillary distance

Contact your eye doctor if you need a copy of your prescription.

You can measure your PD at home:

  1. Stand 8 inches from a mirror
  2. Hold a ruler against your brow
  3. Close your right eye and align the ruler's 0mm with your left pupil center
  4. Close your left eye and open your right
  5. Note the measurement to your right pupil center

Average adult PD is between 54-74mm. You can also ask your eye doctor for this measurement.
